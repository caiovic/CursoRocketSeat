## Podemos usar o shorthand background para definir todos os valores do background

```Css
background-color: rgb(55 90 110);
background-image: url(https://seeklogo.com/images/W/whatsapp-icon-logo-BDC0A8063B-seeklogo.com.png);
background-repeat: no-repeat;
background-position: center;
background-size: contain;
background-size: 30% auto; 
background-origin: content-box;  
background-clip: padding-box;
```


## Shorthand dos comandos acima:

```Css

background: rgb(55 90 110) url(https://seeklogo.com/images/W/whatsapp-icon-logo-BDC0A8063B-seeklogo.com.png) no-repeat center contain / 30% auto content-box padding-box scroll;
